01 — About

What is The Last Chance?

The Last Chance is a Web3 platform built for one purpose: turning NFT mints into direct, trackable donations for causes that rarely get funded in time.

We don't run one campaign forever. Each campaign has a start, a confirmed foundation partner on the ground, a funding goal, and an end — followed by a full public report of where every dollar went.

Our first campaign, Maggot Rescue, funds emergency treatment for street dogs suffering from untreated wounds and infections, in partnership with a confirmed local foundation.

02 — Current Campaign

Campaign 01

Giving Forgotten Street Dogs a Second Chance at Life

Every day, street dogs suffer from open wounds, maggot infestations, and infections that go untreated simply because no one steps in first. Maggot Rescue funds the emergency medical care that changes that outcome — antiseptic treatment, wound care, and recovery support for dogs who would otherwise be left to die on the street.

04 — Founder Vision

Why this exists

The Last Chance started from a simple frustration: watching charity campaigns raise money with no real way to verify where it went. This platform exists to fix that — every mint, every wallet movement, and every report will be public, permanent, and checkable by anyone.

I'm not taking a cut from this first campaign. The goal is to prove the model works before asking anyone to trust it with more.

— Kai Lim, Founder
